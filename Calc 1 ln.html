Formulas

dy/dx (ln(x))=1/x × x′

Example 1

Find the derivative of y=ln(3x+5)

Step 1

First, Put the function in the parentheses under 1:

1/3x+5

Step 2

Next, multiply the fraction by the derivative of the original function in parentheses:

1/3x+5 × 3

Step 3

Once you do this, simplify to get your final answer:

3/(3x+5)
