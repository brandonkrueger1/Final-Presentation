Derivatives of Logarithmic Functions

If aa is a positive real number and a ≠ 1, then:


d/dx log↓a (x) = (1)/xln(a)

Example:

log↓10 ((x+1)/x)


1/((x+1/x)ln(10)) ⋅ d/dx ((x+1)/x)


x/x+1)ln(10) ⋅ ((x-(x+1)/x2)


ANSWER: -1/(x(x+1)ln(10)
