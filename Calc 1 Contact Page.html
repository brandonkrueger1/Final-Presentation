FAQ's

Do we plan on expanding into other subjects?

Yes we are, Currently we are discussing creating lessons for integrals!

 

Why did we choose derivatives?

We chose derivatives becuase we felt that they were a good starting point for tutoring calculus. Since their are so many types of derivatives, we wanted to create an easy and helpful guide anyone can use.

 

What was our inspiration for creating this website?

We decided to create this website as a final project for our web development class. This class was linked with our calculus course, so we decided to combine the two and help our fellow students.
