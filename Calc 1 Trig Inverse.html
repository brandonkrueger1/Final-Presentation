Formulas

dy/dx (sin-1s(x)) = x′/√(1-x2)   dy/dy (cos-1(x)) = -x′/√(1-x2)
dy/dx (tan-1(x)) = x′/(1+x2)   dy/dx (sec-1(x)) = x′/lxl√(x2-1)
dy/dx (csc-1(x)) = -x′/lxl√(x2-1)   dy/dx (cot-1(x)) = -x′/(1+x2

Example

Find the derivative of y = tan-1(2x-9)

Step 1

First, take the derivative of the function in the parentheses:

x

Step 2

Next, divide the derivative by (1+(original function in parentheses)2):

x/(1+(2x-9)2)

Step 3

Next, simplify the denominator to get your final answer:

x/(4x2-32x+19)
